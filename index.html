Background Information

Apple's iOS 18 is Apple's latest and biggest software update ever! It is packed with so many new features like: a customizable control center, Apple Intelligence, new look for app icons, rearrangement of apps and widgets, locking and hiding apps, a controls gallery for the control center and your lock screen, and so much more! Not only is iOS 18 packed with new features, but it also comes with a ton of security enhancements, bug fixes, and so many more enhancements to the OS!
